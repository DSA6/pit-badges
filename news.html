⚠️ Safety Tips for PIT Operations

Posted: March 6, 2026

The following are key safety instructions for Powered Industrial Truck (PIT) operations:

🔒 Only Trained & Authorized Persons

Only trained and authorized persons can operate a Powered Industrial Truck.

📏 Maintain Minimum Distance

PIT and pedestrians should keep a minimum of 2 meters distance in all directions at all times (unless there is a fixed barrier in place).

🅿️ Designated Parking Only

PIT should only be parked in designated parking areas to avoid unnecessary space reductions and additional obstacles for other PIT operators.

🔧 Use Approved Safety Equipment

Only approved safety knives should be used to cut shrink wrap, and users must wear approved safety gloves.

🦺 Personal Protective Equipment

High visibility clothing and safety boots (high top) must be worn at all times when on the shop floor.

📯 Use Horn for Warnings

PIT operators must use their horn to warn pedestrians and other PIT drivers when turning corners.
